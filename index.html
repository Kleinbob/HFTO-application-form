About HTFO

Help the Future Organisation — a Non-Governmental Organisation dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children in Wakiso District, Uganda.

Who We Are

Our Story

Help the Future Organisation (HTFO) is a registered Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO) based in Nabbingo, Tegga Village, Wakiso District, Uganda. Founded out of a deep concern for the welfare of children left behind by poverty, loss, and hardship, HTFO has grown into a vital pillar of hope for hundreds of families.

We work with orphaned, vulnerable, and disadvantaged children — providing educational support, basic needs assistance, psychosocial counselling, and family strengthening programmes. Our approach is rooted in the belief that every child, regardless of their background, deserves a safe, nurturing environment and the opportunity to achieve their full potential.

Located in the heart of Wakiso District, our reach extends across neighbouring communities, working alongside local leaders, schools, churches, and volunteers to ensure no child is left behind.

🌱 Our Mission

To empower vulnerable and orphaned children in Uganda by providing access to education, basic needs, and a supportive community environment that enables them to grow into productive, self-reliant citizens.

🔭 Our Vision

A Uganda where every child — regardless of their circumstances — has the opportunity to thrive, learn, and contribute meaningfully to society and their community.

What We Do

Our Programmes

📚

Educational Support

School fees, scholastic materials, and mentorship to keep children enrolled and learning.

🍽️

Basic Needs

Nutritional support, clothing, and medical assistance for children in the greatest need.

🏠

Family Strengthening

Supporting guardians and caregivers to provide stable, loving homes for children.

🧠

Psychosocial Care

Counselling and trauma support for orphaned and vulnerable children across the district.
